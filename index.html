Yet-another-new-tab-page

Yet another new tab page which increases your productivity, customises the browser to your tastes and does not have any redundant stuff.

Customise the add-on to your taste by clicking the wrench icon in the bottom right corner.

  • Customise your name displayed on the page
  • Change your background by entering the url to the image
  • Customise your layout
  • Add or delete tiles
  • Have a different icon for every tile
  • Carry your data. Export and Import for any web browser on any platform with a click of a button

Installation and Usage

Chrome

Download it from the web store

Firefox

Download it from the add-on store

Web app

Set the following URL as your default new tab page - https://yet-another-new-tab-page.appspot.com
